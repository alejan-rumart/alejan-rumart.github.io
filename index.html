Selected Projects

Application of Deep Learning for Medical Image Classification (MSc Thesis)

PadChest (PA chest X-rays) • Multi-label CNNs • Discrimination, calibration & operating points

  • Data: PadChest PA radiographs with multi-label findings; strong class imbalance.
  • Approach: Compared transfer-learning backbones (e.g., MobileNetV2/EfficientNet) and training regimes; prioritized weighted BCE, moderate oversampling, and CutMix; reported Grad-CAM for interpretability.
  • Result: Training procedure mattered more than backbone family; macro AUC-ROC/PR-AUC improved with CutMix; reported Brier calibration and clinically useful Spec@Sens=95% / Sens@Spec=95% points.

Alphasky — Exoplanet Sky Explorer (NASA Space Apps Challenge)

Educational app: how the night sky looks from other exoplanets • For mid/high-school classrooms

  • Processed public datasets (NASA Exoplanet Archive; stars from Gaia DR3) to render skies from ~20 accessible exoplanets.
  • Ursina (Python) engine; interactive 3D starfield, light-pollution simulation, manual constellation builder, astronaut guide UI.

3D Orientational Imaging of Gallbladder Stones (BSc Thesis)

X-ray Diffraction Tensor Tomography (XRD-TT) + Propagation Phase-Contrast CT (PPC-CT)

  • Problem: Non-destructive mapping of microstructure and crystal orientation in gallstones.
  • Approach: Synchrotron data (ESRF ID15A); reconstructed voxel-wise scattering tensors with spherical harmonics; compared TT volume to PPC-CT morphology.
  • Result: Confirmed cholesterol monohydrate composition and preferred orientations (edge-parallel long axes), consistent with layered morphology and internal cavities.
